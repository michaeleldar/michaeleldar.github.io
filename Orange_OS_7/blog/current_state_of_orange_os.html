Current state of Orange OS

29th Of April by @applejuiceproduc

In Orange OS, at the time of writing, the OS contains signing in, creating an account, a pen platformer game
and a marketplace app. We also have a text editor app, a camera app, a tips app, a calculator app
and flappy bird, but these aren't included in Orange OS main yet.
